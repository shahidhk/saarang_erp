Greetings from the Saarang 2014 team!
 
Saarang, South India's largest cultural festival is scheduled to be held fromJanuary 8th to 12th this year. With over 50 events, 5 Proshows, and a series of Lectures, Demonstrations, Workshops, and a World Festival to keep you entertained, Saarang 2014 promises to be better than ever. 
 
Here are our spotlight events for this year:
 
Times of India Decibels: The ultimate battle of the bands is back in a whole new avatar, bigger and a whole lot better. Sponsored by The Times of India, the winners of our semi-professional band hunt stand to win:
  • 1.5 Lakhs in prize money
  • A chance to open for the headliners of our Rock Show - Architects at the Open Air Theatre (OAT)
  • A 3 song EP deal with Krimson Avenue studios
  • Gigs at Blue Frog Bombay and Pune. 
Hurry up and send in your registrations to wm@saarang.org by 20th December.
 
Panache: We invite you to Panache- The Style and Fashion Show at Saarang 2014! This year at Panache, BE UNFORMAL.  Sponsored by Vimal, the winners stand to win:
  • Up to Rs. 50,000 in prize money
  • Goodies from Vimal
Deadline for registrations: 25th December. Check out the websitewww.saarang.org or send an email to panache@saarang.org for more details.
 
Djukebox: Vocals, instruments, and some crazy energy. The only thing missing from Saarang was some music to get the crowd grooving and partying. Registration on a first come, first serve basis. Register at www.saarang.org or send an email to wm@saarang.org for more details.

What's at stake: Rs.25,000 and the chance to show your mettle to some of the best in the reporting industry.

Short Film Making & Saarang Film Festival: The next Christopher Nolan? Fame and glory await. Best actors as decided by our jury win workshops with Anupam Kher's Actor Prepares. 
 
Deadline for registrations: 27th December at www.saarang.org
